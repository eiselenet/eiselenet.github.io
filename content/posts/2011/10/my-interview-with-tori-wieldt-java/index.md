Walking around <a href="http://www.oracle.com/javaone" target="_blank">JavaOne</a> exhibition hall was awesome this year. Crowded for nearly the complete day and always people to run into and talk to. Same happened to me with Tori. She is part of the <a href="" target="_blank">Oracle Technology Network</a> (OTN) team and the face behind <a href="http://twitter.com/Java" target="_blank">@Java</a>'s twitter account. We had a great 4 minutes and 07 seconds talk about Java, JavaOne, the community and JavaEE. Thanks for taking the time, Tori. You are doing a great job!
