Have you seen the JBoss AS7 being released yesterday? Nice work! My congratulations to the whole team! Looking forward to the full Java EE 6 profile. There is one thing I am&nbsp;jealous&nbsp;about: They have those fancy wallpapers up in different resolutions. And to me it seems as if the GlassFish community is somehow missing a little bit of visual creative people. To be honest, I'm not the one claiming to be the most creative graphic designer out there, but if there is time, I'll try to do my best. Let me know, what you think!
