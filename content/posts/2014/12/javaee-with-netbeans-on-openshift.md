Today was <a href="http://www.eppleton.de/training/event/64-java/52-netbeans-day/0" target="_blank">NetBeans Day in Munich</a>. I had the pleasure to present one session about the integration of Red Hat's products with my famous IDE. So, I've been talking about <a href="" target="_blank">WildFly</a>, <a href="" target="_blank">EAP</a>, Git, <a href="" target="_blank">OpenShift Online</a> and showed all the different ways optimize your development workflow with that tool-set.
