Justin Kestelyn posted<a href="http://www.oracle.com/technology/syndication/techcasts/index.html" target="_blank"> audio versions of the Oracle OpenWorld Live "TechCast"</a> chats 
<br>
<br>
 Oracle TechCasts are audio podcasts, hosted by OTN's Justin Kestelyn, with technical experts about new tools, technologies, and trends in application development. 
<br>
<br>
 The following two are most relevant for WebLogic Server. 
<br>
<br>
 Nov. 9, 2009, <strong>Oracle OpenWorld Live 2009: Oracle WebLogic Server Looks Toward OSGi</strong>
<br>
 Senior Director Product Management Mike Lehmann details the recent history of WebLogic Server development and also looks toward the future, to OSGi and virtualization.

 Nov. 9, 2009, <strong>Oracle OpenWorld Live 2009: Oracle WebLogic Server On Tap from the Private Cloud</strong>
