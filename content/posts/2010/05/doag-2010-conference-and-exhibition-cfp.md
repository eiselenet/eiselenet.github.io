As you might have heard. The Call for Papers for the DOAG 2010 Conference and Exhibition is still open until 2010/30/06.
<br>
 In more than 300 speakers slots the DOAG 2010 Conference, which takes place November 16th-18th, 2010 in Nuremberg, provides current information on the successful use of the Oracle products as well as practical tips and tricks and exchange of experience. Stay up to date with informations and follow <a href="http://twitter.com/doagkonferenz" target="_blank">@doagkonferenz</a> on twitter.
